About Brittney O'Rourke

I aim higher than the rest, and strive for perfection.

I am a Radio Personality for Beasley Broadcast Group's WWDB-860AM, "Music Talk", in the Greater Philadelphia Area. When I'm not on air, I volunteer with Career Wardrobe Non-profit Organization that provides a continuous system of employ-ability support to meet the needs of women as they transition into the workforce. I've also spoken at Immaculata University to shed light on young scholars interested in my line of work. It was interesting to give positive advice/feedback for the students interested in pursing similar professions so they could succeed in this industry too.


In 2013, I co-hosted Philadelphia Hip Hop Awards and worked alongside some amazing talent in the industry. Currently, I serve as the VP A&R for Ethical Music Entertainment which focuses on music artist Ms.BriaMarie, an incredible Temple University graduate that completed a college tour earlier this year, has had singles such as "IDK" and "Celebrate" placed on VH1's hit reality show Love & Hip-Hop, and has been named the face of Essencia makeup line! Ethical Music Entertainment's CEO and Grammy-Award Winning Songwriter/Producer Carvin Haggins keeps the momentum of the label going as we continue to create feel good music with ethics for the fans.
